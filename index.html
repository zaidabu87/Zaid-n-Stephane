Mark Zuckerberg

The man who created Facebook

Here's a timeline of Mark Zuckerberg]

  • 1984 - Born in White Plains, NY
  • 1995 - First Tries Computer Programming
  • 1999 - Founds His Own Company
  • 2002 - Goes to Harvard
  • 2004 - Creates Facebook in His Dorm Room
  • 2004 - Drops out of Harvard
  • 2007 - Named World's Youngest Billionaire
  • 2010 - Facebook Reaches 500 Million People
  • 2010 - Donates $100 Million Dollars
  • 2010 - Mark's Life is Made into a Movie
